ABOUT LITTLE PAWS

Little Paws is not just a pet home/shelter.
We're a family of passionate staff,
volunteers and supporters committed to enriching the lives of homeless pets from the moment they
enter our care to the moment they find their forever home with you.

Want a Pet For Your
Loved Ones?

Our dogs are the perfect gift for your loved
ones on every occassion. No matter how young or old
they are, there is a puppy for everyone.

BUILDING A LIFE WITH YOUR PET

Whether you are bringing a new pet home, merging multipet households, or simply looking for a playdate-with a shelter pet, our staff is here to help.
